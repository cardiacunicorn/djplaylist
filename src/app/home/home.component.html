Welcome to DJ Playlist

Let's admit it, you've already got an amazing selection of songs on that Spotify playlist you made. But, as good as that groovy 🎵🎷🎶 jazz song is, it can feel out of place when it's played straight after that sleeper punk hit 🥁🔥🎸

DJ Playlist allows you to log in to your Spotify account, grab that playlist, and re-order those great songs into a playlist that makes more sense. No more shuffle, no more skipping songs when they cause the mood to shift 😩🤮

The advanced metrics 📈 provided for each song means that you can curate your playlists for the context your play them in: be it a dancefloor 💃 going for a run 🏃‍♀️ or just ramping up from obscure indie 🪕🎹 to the latest pop 👩‍🎤🎤

You'll understand better once you see these metrics 😉
